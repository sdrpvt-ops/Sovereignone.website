Overview

Structured across three complementary strategies

SovereignOne Capital deploys capital across a defined set of strategies, each selected for its ability to capture value through structure, timing, and disciplined execution. The platform is designed to operate across both emerging and traditional markets, with a focus on alignment, flexibility, and risk-aware positioning.

Digital Assets

Focused on structured exposure to digital assets, including Bitcoin and related opportunities. The strategy prioritises capital preservation, disciplined entry, and long-term positioning in a rapidly evolving market.

Real Assets

Deployment of capital into tangible, asset-backed opportunities, including aviation and property-linked strategies. Emphasis is placed on downside protection, structured transactions, and predictable value creation.

Venture Capital

Selective investment into early and growth-stage businesses where capital can be paired with strategic support. Focused on opportunities with strong fundamentals, scalable models, and clear pathways to value creation.

Platform Integration

Connected to the wider Group

SovereignOne Capital operates both independently and alongside other Group activities, enabling flexible capital deployment across the broader platform.
