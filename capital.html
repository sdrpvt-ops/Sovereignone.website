Overview

The structuring layer of SovereignOne

SovereignOne Capital supports the Group by sourcing, structuring, and aligning funding for trading strategies, asset management opportunities, and selective strategic investments.

The focus is not simply raising capital, but matching capital with clearly defined opportunities where structure, access, timing, and execution can create differentiated outcomes.

Capital Model

Integrated across the platform

CAPITAL → TRADING → ASSET MANAGEMENT → SCALE

Capital is structured and raised, then deployed into trading strategies and asset-backed opportunities. Trading can generate liquidity and transaction evidence, while Asset Management converts selected capital into longer-term value across property, aviation, and digital assets.

Focus Areas

Capital structuring across three core areas

Investor Capital

Relationship-led funding structures for investors seeking exposure to SovereignOne strategies across trading, asset management, and strategic growth opportunities.

Digital Asset Holders

Structured solutions for crypto holders seeking liquidity, yield, collateralisation, custody-based strategies, or asset-backed participation without necessarily disposing of core holdings.

Strategic Finance

Bespoke financing solutions supporting trading transactions, property opportunities, aviation assets, and wider Group capital deployment.

Platform Integration

Connected to Trading and Asset Management

Capital is the funding and structuring layer of SovereignOne. It supports Trading by enabling transaction funding and supports Asset Management by providing capital pathways into property, aviation, and digital asset strategies.

This creates a joined-up model where funding, execution, and asset management are not treated as separate activities, but as connected parts of one platform.

Opportunities are typically sourced through existing relationships, proprietary access, and aligned partnerships. Participation is selective and generally by introduction.
